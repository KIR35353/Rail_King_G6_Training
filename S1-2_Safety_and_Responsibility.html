Safety and Responsibility
Section 1
Role Responsibilities
👤
Operator
Follow approved procedures. Report abnormal conditions immediately. Do not attempt repairs beyond role boundary.
🔧
Service Technician
Apply model-specific service manual before any corrective action. Cross-reference OEM documentation where specified.
📋
Supervisor / Lead
Confirm role boundary is observed. Authorise escalation to support when conditions are unclear or unsafe.
Non-Negotiable Safety Rules
Stop at uncertainty. If a procedure result is unclear, stop and escalate. Do not guess.
No undocumented actions. Any corrective step must be traceable to an approved procedure or directive.
Document all abnormal conditions. Verbal communication is not sufficient — use the equipment tech support form.
Role limits apply always. Operators do not perform service-level tasks. Technicians do not operate without model verification.
Escalation is never optional. When safe operating limits are uncertain, escalating immediately is the correct action.

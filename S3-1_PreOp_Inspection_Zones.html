Pre-Operation Inspection Zones
Section 3
Systematic Inspection Sequence
Zone 1: Perimeter & Safety
Clear walkways and obstacles
Safety guards and covers in place
No foreign materials or hazards
Zone 2: Fluid Levels & Containment
Fuel level within operating range
Oil level at mark
Coolant level and condition
No visible leaks
Zone 3: Control Panel & Interface
Display powered and operational
Control switches in neutral/off
No error lights or warnings
Zone 4: External Systems
Hoses and connections secure
No visible damage or wear
Environmental conditions noted

Pre-Ignition Readiness Sequence
Perform these checks in order at the panel before engaging the starter. Confirm each step before advancing.
1
Master Power On
Engage master electrical switch. Wait for panel to initialise and all warning lamps to complete their self-test cycle.
2
Confirm Neutral / Park
Verify transmission selector in neutral or park. Park brake must be set before proceeding.
⚠ Starter will not engage if not in neutral on most models.
3
Check Air Reservoirs
Primary and secondary gauges must be at operating pressure or able to build to pressure before brake release.
4
Verify No Active Faults
Any persistent fault lamp (red or amber) must be resolved or accepted per model manual before startup proceeds.
⚠ Do not clear a fault by resetting the panel — identify the cause first.
5
Confirm Clear Zone
Sound horn once. Ensure all personnel are clear of the machine before engaging the starter.

Confirm the action is within approved procedure scope.

Common Warranty Boundary Situations
Replacing a listed consumable per schedule: covered.
Adjusting a component to a non-specified setting: review required.
Bypassing a safety interlock: coverage voided.
Running unit with known unreported fault: coverage at risk.
Key principle: Warranty boundaries are not bureaucratic hurdles — they exist to ensure that every action taken on the equipment is traceable, safe, and technically sound.

Powertrain, Brake and Auxiliary References
Section 6
Powertrain and Auxiliary OEM Documents
JOHN DEERE
Engine — CTM43 Component Technical Manual
Doc 08 — John Deere 1200/1400 Series CTM43
Full component-level manual covering fuel system, electrical system, mechanical diagnosis, and torque specifications for the engine fitted to the RK24. Use for fuel injection, governor, and sensor diagnostics. John Deere engine warranty work must be performed by an authorised JD dealer.
FUNK
Gearboxes — CTM147 and CTM157
Docs 05a–05b — Funk CTM147 and CTM157
Two Funk gearbox variants used in the RK24 drivetrain. Covers gear ratios, lubricant type and fill procedure, bearing preload, and assembly torque values. Match the doc to the serial number range on the gearbox data plate — do not cross-apply specs between CTM147 and CTM157.
CTD
Hub-Wheel Assembly
Doc 04 — CTD Hub-Wheel Assembly and Maintenance Manual
Planetary hub reduction on driven axles. Use for bearing preload adjustment, seal replacement, and end-play measurement. Includes torque sequence for hub-cap fasteners and oil fill procedure.
AUX
Cab and Auxiliary Systems
Docs 02, 10, 13, 14, 16 — Mounts, Heater, DEF, Steller, Williams
AMC Marine mount (vibration isolation), Red Dot R-295 cab heater, Shaw DEF tank service, Steller V480 auxiliary regulator, Williams WM786 component. Consult the relevant doc for any auxiliary system fault before ordering parts.
When to Use These Documents
Engine Question
The engine is starting hard in cold conditions and showing a fuel system fault code. Where do I look?
John Deere CTM43 (Doc 08) — fuel system section. Check the injection pump delivery pressure spec and cold-start enrichment circuit. If the fault code is engine-specific, note the CTM43 section reference before calling the JD dealer.
Gearbox Question
Gearbox oil sample shows high metal particle count at service. What's the procedure?
Identify gearbox variant from data plate. Open CTM147 or CTM157 (Doc 05a or 05b) — locate the inspection/disassembly section. Metal contamination triggers a full bearing and gear inspection before any service interval reset.
Auxiliary System Question
The DEF system fault lamp is on and the machine is approaching emissions derate. What documents are needed?
Shaw DEF Tank Service Manual (Doc 13) — check tank sensor, heater, and supply line for fault source. John Deere CTM43 for the derate threshold and any engine-side DEF management procedure.
Warranty boundary reminder: Engine work (John Deere CTM43) must be performed by an authorised JD service dealer to preserve engine warranty. All other powertrain components in this section are within the Rail King service scope — use the relevant OEM doc and the appropriate forms.
Match the gearbox variant before opening any procedure. CTM147 and CTM157 are not interchangeable — verify from the data plate on the unit.

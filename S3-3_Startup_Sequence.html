Cold-Start Considerations
In ambient temperatures below 5 °C, extend the low-idle warm-up period. Hydraulic oil viscosity increases significantly in cold conditions — premature loading risks pump cavitation. Consult model manual for minimum fluid temp before actuation.
Oil Pressure Failure at Gate 4
If oil pressure does not rise within 10 seconds: shut down immediately. Do not attempt a second start. Check oil level, look for visible leaks, and escalate to service before restarting.
Never Skip the Warm-Up Gate
Engaging the travel or work circuits while coolant and hydraulic temps are below operating range is the leading cause of premature seal and pump wear on Gen6 equipment.
Three gates — all must pass: Gate 1 = pre-op complete. Gate 2 = panel clear, neutral confirmed. Gate 3 = warm-up done, all systems in range. Do not bypass a gate under time pressure.

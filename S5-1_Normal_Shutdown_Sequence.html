Normal Shutdown Sequence
Section 5
Controlled Shutdown Procedure
Step 1: Load Reduction
Gradually reduce operating load to idle over 5–10 minutes. Monitor temperature and pressure descent.
Step 2: Idle Stabilization
Run at idle for 2–3 minutes minimum. Confirm all indicators stable and normal before proceeding.
Step 3: Control Shutdown
Use control panel shutdown command or key sequence per model manual. Do not force power off.
Step 4: Verify Shutdown
Confirm all indicator lights extinguish and no audible operation occurs. Wait 30 seconds before next action.
⚠ If unit does not shut down cleanly, escalate immediately. Do not force disconnect.
Step 5: Control Securing
Lock or secure control switches per facility protocol to prevent accidental restart.

What Makes Data Quality High
Specific and observable
"Hydraulic pressure dropped to 1800 PSI at 09:42 during load cycle 3." Gives the support team exact facts.
Includes steps already taken
List actions performed and their result. Prevents duplicate troubleshooting cycles.
Vague descriptions
"It was making a noise." — Requires call-backs, wastes time, delays resolution.
Missing unit identification
No model/serial means the support team cannot pull the correct service history or documentation.
Data quality rule: Every field on the form exists because support teams need it to act quickly. Incomplete forms directly delay resolution.
